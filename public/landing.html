Features

🎙

Local Transcription

Whisper runs in your browser via WebAssembly. Word-level timestamps, no server required.

🔒

Privacy First

Nothing leaves your browser. No uploads, no accounts, no telemetry. Works offline as a PWA.

🖥

Open Source

MIT licensed. Hack it, fork it, embed it. No credit meters, no subscription tiers, no vendor lock-in.

✂

Delete-Only Editing

Select words in the transcript, press delete. That segment is removed from the video. Simple and predictable.

⚡

Chrome Export

Export to WebM (VP9 + Opus) via the WebCodecs API. Hardware-accelerated encode on Chrome and Edge.

💰

Free Forever

No paywalls, no usage limits, no credit metering. Transcribe as much as your hardware can handle.

Current Limitations

These are deliberate MVP scope decisions. They are documented, not hidden.

Chrome/Edge only for export Delete-only — no insert or replace WebM output only (no MP4) English only (more languages in Phase 2) No AI voice cloning No AI audio enhancement No screen recording No multi-track editing
